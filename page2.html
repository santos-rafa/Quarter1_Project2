CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Kapitan Tiago
  • He is a business man in San Diego and agreed his daughter to marry Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • Husband of Donya Pia Alba
Elias
  • Elias used to be a fugitive in San Diego, he would work so he could improve the society he was in.
  • He was saved by Crisostomo Ibarra.
Sisa
  • Sisa is the mother of Basilio and Crispin.
  • Wife of Pedro.
Tiya Isabel
  • Tiya Isabel is the caretaker of Maria Clara
  • Aunt of Maria Clara
  • Cousin of Kapitan Tiyago
